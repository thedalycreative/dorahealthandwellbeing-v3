Our Founder

Meet George

A Nurse by Nature, A Leader by Heart

George
Founder & Director
Hocking, Perth WA

Even from a young age, George's path seemed clear. As a toddler, barely two or three years old, she was already embracing the role — playfully donning a nurse's outfit and earning the affectionate nickname "Nurse Georgina." This early inclination foreshadowed a lifelong passion for caring for others.

For many years, George honed her skills working with private clients requiring specialist care and high levels of support. She quickly discovered a natural aptitude for this demanding field. "Being a nurse for a patient with paraplegia and then quadriplegia — I've got the knack for it, or so I'm told," she shares, highlighting her unique ability to connect with and effectively care for those with complex needs.

In 2023, George and her husband made the significant move from England to Perth, Western Australia, with their newborn daughter, Thea. The decision was rooted in a desire for Thea to grow up close to family. They instantly fell in love with Perth, settling north of the river.

It was shortly after their move that George identified a critical gap in Perth's healthcare landscape. "A client I had specialist care for pointed out the gap here in Perth. WA has this gap in the market where you can't get a good, decent, reliable, helpful support worker — you are lucky to get one with experience at all!"

Driven by this observation and her profound commitment to quality care, George founded DORA Healthcare — named after her daughter, Theadora, symbolising the personal heart and soul poured into its mission.

"I am lucky to be both happy and successful with my career. DORA has got the best healthcare workers I can find in Perth — myself included."
— George, Founder
